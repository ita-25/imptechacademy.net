Bridging the Digital Divide

At Impact Tech Academy, we saw a gap in the world of technology education. Many aspiring students are held back by financial constraints or a lack of access to expensive equipment like laptops. We believe that talent and ambition should not be limited by circumstance.

Our academy was founded on the principle that **technology is for everyone**. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, foundational tech training that is both affordable and accessible. Our groundbreaking "phone-first" approach for the Activate Tech 1.0 program is a testament to this commitment, ensuring that anyone with a smartphone can begin their journey into the tech world.

Our Mission: Bringing Tech to all

The goal for our **Activate Tech 1.0** program is simple: to get people started in tech, regardless of their background or financial situation. We believe that technology is for everyone, and our mission is to empower you to start with the tools you already have.

The truth is, if you have an Android phone, you have a powerful tool for technology in your hands. Most people are unaware of its immense potential, and we are dedicated to helping them unlock it. **Activate Tech 1.0** is our first step in proving that you have all the tech you need to begin your journey and create amazing things at a very low price.

Our Vision

Impact Tech Academy envisions to raise tech giants. We believe there are many tech giants who don't know they are, and our goal is to provide them with the right motivation and drive to get started.
