Our Mission: Tech for all

Empowering the next generation of innovators through accessible education.

Bridging the Digital Divide with Activate Tech 1.0

The goal for our **Activate Tech 1.0** program is simple: to get people started in tech, regardless of their background or financial situation. We know many aspiring tech enthusiasts are held back by the financial constraints of buying a laptop or the belief that they need expensive equipment to get started. We're here to change that.

The truth is, if you have an Android phone, you already have a powerful tool for technology in your hands. Most people are unaware of the immense potential of their phones, and we're dedicated to helping them unlock it. **Activate Tech 1.0** is our first step in proving that you have all the tech you need to begin your journey and create amazing things.
