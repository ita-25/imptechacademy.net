Our Mission: Bringing Tech to all

Impact Tech Academy believes every motivated individual deserves a start in tech. Our **Activate Tech 1.0** program provides foundational web development skills (HTML, CSS, JS) using highly accessible tools like Android phones, eliminating major financial barriers.

Your sponsorship doesn't just fund a course—it funds a career. It directly helps us cover operational costs, provide training materials, and expand our reach to marginalized communities.

Partner with Impact: Fueling the Next Generation of Innovators

At Impact Tech Academy, we tear down the barriers to tech education. Our mission is to equip aspiring developers—especially those with limited resources—with job-ready skills, starting with the very device they already own: their smartphone. Your partnership is the essential engine that turns potential into progress.

Our Essential Needs & Immediate Impact

Every contribution directly enhances our capacity to deliver high-quality, accessible tech education across Nigeria and beyond.

A Vision for Tomorrow: Future Needs & High-Tech Leverage
