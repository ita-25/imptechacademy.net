Our Mission: Bringing Tech to all

Impact Tech Academy believes every motivated individual deserves a start in tech. Our **Activate Tech 1.0** program provides foundational web development skills (HTML, CSS, JS) using highly accessible tools like Android phones, eliminating major financial barriers.

Your sponsorship doesn't just fund a course—it funds a career. It directly helps us cover operational costs, provide training materials, and expand our reach to marginalized communities.
