Frequently Asked Questions

We welcome individuals who are:

  • Between the ages of 18 and 35
  • Passionate about technology, innovation, and community development
  • Able to commit at least 5 hours per week
  • Located in Nigeria or able to work remotely if applicable
  • Event Organization (workshops, bootcamps, training sessions)
  • Community Management (supporting new members, answering questions)
  • Tech Education (assisting with tutorials, webinars, or mentorship)
  • Content Creation (articles, social media, graphics, videos)
  • Logistics and Admin Support

We expect volunteers to commit at least 5 hours per week for a minimum of 6 months. This helps us maintain consistency and long-term impact.
